🏋️‍♂️ Types of Gyms


Commercial Gyms – Open to the public with membership fees (e.g., Planet Fitness, Gold’s Gym).

Fitness Studios – Specialized in one area like yoga, spin, CrossFit, or Pilates.

Home Gyms – Personal setups with weights, mats, or machines.

Boxing/Martial Arts Gyms – Focused on combat sports and fitness.

Functional Fitness/CrossFit Boxes – Emphasize strength, conditioning, and mobility.

🧠 Key Areas of Fitness

Cardio – Treadmill, cycling, rowing, running, etc.

Strength Training – Free weights, machines, resistance bands.

Flexibility & Mobility – Stretching, yoga, foam rolling.

Core Work – Planks, crunches, stability exercises.

🧭 Beginner Gym Routine (3 Days a Week)


Day 1: Upper Body (Push/Pull – chest, back, arms)

Day 2: Lower Body (squats, lunges, leg press)

Day 3: Full Body or Cardio + Core

🧴 What to Bring

Water bottle

Towel

Workout clothes & shoes

Headphones

Lock (if using a locker)

✅ Basic Gym Etiquette


Wipe down equipment after use

Re-rack weights

Don't hog machines

Respect personal space

Keep phone use minimal
